Define the income your designed life requires. Understand where you stand. Know what to do next.

The Fulfillment Number™ calculates the exact monthly income your designed life requires — at both a minimum stability level and an optimal level. Enter your allocations across housing, food, transport, health, lifestyle, and growth. The tool returns your Essential Number, your Optimal Number, and a precise gap analysis against your current income.
No personal data is collected. All calculations run in your browser.
